Shipping & Returns

Shipping

All Rawhide City Leather pieces are made to order, by hand, one at a time.

Lead Time: 6 weeks from order to your door, every piece.

Shipping Cost: $10 flat rate to anywhere in the continental United States via UPS or USPS. Free shipping on orders over $150. We currently do not ship internationally.

Tracking: You'll receive a tracking number by email the moment your order ships.

Warranty & Returns

All Rawhide City Leather products come with a career warranty. Any item with a defect in workmanship or materials will be repaired by us.

Because every piece is made by hand and most are made to order, all sales are final. Custom and personalized orders cannot be returned or exchanged. If your order arrives damaged or with a defect from our workshop, contact us within 7 days of delivery and we'll make it right: repair, replace, or refund.

To make a warranty claim, email us at rawhidecityleather@gmail.com with your order number and photos of the issue. We'll get back to you within a few days with next steps.

Please Note

Leather is a natural product and variations in finishes can and will occur. All of our work is done by hand and not mass production, so there can be minor variations in pictures vs. what is received. We strive for perfection and will not send an item out that we do not approve of.

Care & Upkeep

Leather products do require care and up-keep in order to get the full life from the item. We encourage you to purchase our leather balm and condition the leather as well as clean with saddle soap.

Any hand-painted customization must be handled appropriately. Excessive bending, rubbing, scraping, or abrasion will not only crack and damage the paint, it will damage the leather itself.

Radio Strap Disclaimer

Take care of your radio strap and its hand-painted letters. Avoid constantly bending, folding, or creasing the strap, especially across the painted lettering. Constant bending will crack the paint and damage the leather itself. When your strap is not in use, store it flat or hang it up.

Improved Accessibility & Reception: A radio strap positions the radio's antenna away from the body for better signal reception and keeps the microphone accessible on the chest, even when wearing bulky turnout gear.

Comfort & Ergonomics: Our straps use wide, thick leather to distribute the weight of the radio across the shoulder, reducing strain during long shifts.

Durability & Tradition: Leather is highly heat-resistant, provides long-term durability, and serves as a long-standing symbol of tradition within the fire service.

Use Disclaimer

Our gear is built for convenience on the job: keeping your equipment organized, within reach, and easier to work with. It is not a replacement for approved safety equipment and should never be relied on to protect you from any hazard.
