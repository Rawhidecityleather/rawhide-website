Shipping & Returns

Shipping

All Rawhide City Leather pieces are made to order, by hand, one at a time.

Lead Time: 6 weeks from order to your door, every piece.

Shipping Cost: $10 flat rate to anywhere in the continental United States via USPS. Free shipping on orders over $175. We currently do not ship internationally.

Tracking: You'll receive a tracking number by email the moment your order ships.

Warranty & Returns

All Box 31 Leatherworks products come with a career warranty. Any item that has any workmanship or material defects will be repaired by us.

Because every piece is made by hand and most are made to order, all sales are final. Custom and personalized orders cannot be returned or exchanged. If your order arrives damaged or with a defect from our workshop, contact us within 7 days of delivery and we'll make it right: repair, replace, or refund.

To make a warranty claim, email us at rawhidecityleather@gmail.com with your order number and photos of the issue. We'll get back to you within a few days with next steps.

Please Note

Leather is a natural product and variations in finishes can and will occur. All of our work is done by hand and not mass production, so there can be minor variations in pictures vs. what is received. We strive for perfection and will not send an item out that we do not approve of.

Care & Upkeep

Leather products do require care and up-keep in order to get the full life from the item. We encourage you to purchase our leather balm and condition the leather as well as clean with saddle soap.

Any paint or Hi-Def customization must be handled appropriately. Excessive bending, rubbing, scraping, or abrasion of leather will not only cause cracking and damage to paint and Hi-Def customization, but it will also damage the leather itself.

Hi-Def & Radio Strap Disclaimer

Hi-Def is NOT stamped and painted. The descriptions and examples make that very clear, but we feel we need to add this into the disclaimer also.

Radio Straps are intended to be worn on the inside of your jacket, whether on a fire scene or an accident. The purpose of the radio strap is to protect the mic cord from heat, sharp objects, and from becoming an entanglement hazard. This is only done by wearing the radio strap on the inside of the jacket. This will also aid in keeping any Hi-Def customization protected from damage.
