POLICIES

Privacy Policy

Last updated June 29, 2026

Rawhide City Leather ("we," "us," "our") respects your privacy. This page explains what information we collect when you visit rawhidecitylthr.com or place an order, how we use it, and who we share it with. We're a small, firefighter-owned shop based in Lakeland, Florida — we don't sell your information, and we only collect what we need to make and ship your gear.

Information We Collect

Order information: name, shipping address, email, phone number, and payment details. Payment is processed by our checkout provider, Snipcart, and its payment partners — we never see or store your full card number.

Custom order details: if you order a custom piece, we collect whatever you give us to build it — names, station numbers, badge designs, color choices, and similar details to be stamped or stitched into your gear.

Contact information: anything you send us through the contact form or by email.

Site usage: basic technical information like browser type and pages visited, collected automatically to help us understand how the site is used and fix problems.

How We Use It

We use your information to process and ship your order, communicate with you about that order, build custom pieces to your spec, respond to questions, and improve the site. If you've opted in to hear from us, we may occasionally send updates about new products — you can unsubscribe at any time.

Who We Share It With

We share order information only with the third parties needed to fulfill it: our checkout and payment processor (Snipcart and its payment partners) and shipping carriers (such as USPS) to deliver your order. We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to anyone for marketing purposes.

Cookies

Our checkout widget (Snipcart) uses cookies to keep track of what's in your cart. These are functional and don't track you across other websites for advertising purposes.

Data Retention & Security

We keep order records as long as needed for tax, warranty, and legal purposes, and delete or anonymize information we no longer need. We take reasonable steps to protect your information, but no online system is 100% secure, and we can't guarantee absolute security.

Your Choices

You can ask us to access, correct, or delete the personal information we hold about you at any time by emailing rawhidecityleather@gmail.com. We'll respond within a reasonable time.

Children's Privacy

This site is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date at the top.
