OUR STORY

Born from the Firehouse

Rawhide City Leather is a firefighter-owned shop founded in 2024. Every piece is hand-cut, hand-stamped, and finished by hand in Lakeland, Florida. No outsourcing, no mass production, no shortcuts. Just full-grain leather, honest work, and gear built to ride out the long shifts.
